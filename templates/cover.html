Black Publications in America

Mapping the History of Black Press (1859–2025)

About This Project

This interactive visualization documents over 165 years of Black press history in the United States. This application considers Black Publications published between 1859 and 2025. 20 magazines I was capable of compiling together, mainly for accessibility access outside of an institution. From the Harlem Renaissance to the Black Power Movement, the voices of African Americans have been represented in various accounts and have strengthened the voices of the voiceless. I dedicate this visualization to those who remained resilient, advocated, and believed in a brighter tomorrow for the race and representation of African Americans in media.
